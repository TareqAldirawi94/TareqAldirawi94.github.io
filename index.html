Research

Multi-Label Classification using Multiple Hypothesis Testing

Conformal FWER
  • Developed distribution-free multi-label classification algorithm combining conformal prediction with multiple hypothesis testing
  • Validated on benchmark datasets demonstrating superior precision-recall tradeoff while maintaining family-wise error rate control

Non-Monotonicity in Conformal Risk Control

Risk Control Theory
  • Extended conformal risk control to settings where the loss function violates the standard monotonicity assumption
  • Generalized the framework to handle distribution shift (covariate and label shift) and demonstrated empirically that risk monotonization yields tighter prediction sets than loss monotonization.

Conditional Guarantee in Conformal Prediction and Risk Control

Conformal Coverage
  • Developed methods to achieve conditional coverage guarantees in conformal prediction beyond marginal validity
  • Validated through simulation studies demonstrating improved conditional coverage using weighted conformal p-values
